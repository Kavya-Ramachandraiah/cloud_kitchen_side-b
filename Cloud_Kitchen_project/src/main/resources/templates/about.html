Project Description

This project aims to create an innovative solution to streamline business processes and improve productivity. Our approach leverages the latest technology to ensure efficiency and user satisfaction. We are committed to delivering a high-quality product that meets the needs of our stakeholders and exceeds their expectations.
Our platform facilitates registration for both customers and caterers. Customers can place orders by specifying their preferred dishes, selecting a caterer, and indicating the delivery details along with the number of servings required. After submission, the caterer evaluates the order history to determine the price, which the customer then pays.
